* Orpheus

Named after the character in greek myth, Orpheus is any and all means of storage that contain my MP3 files. All files are grouped by the music's meaning, from either the lyrics, context or feelings.

The 22 folders present are all major arcana of tarot. File names are 8 characters or shorter to fit my MP3 player's small monochrome LCD when I'm listening to the music. Good luck figuring out the titles!

The files come tagged with name, artist, album, genre, track number and disc number. I mostly use rateyourmusic.com and submithub.com to figure out the genre.

Feel welcome to check my Orpheus on Google Drive.
