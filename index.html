* Orpheus

Named after the character in greek myth, Orpheus is any and all storage devices that contain my MP3 files. All of them are sorted by their meanings, from either the lyrics, context or vibes alone.

The 22 folders present are all major arcana tarot cards. File names are 8 characters or shorter due to the device's small display, such that it can only fit up to that many characters. Good luck figuring them out!

The files come tagged with name, artist, album, genre, track number and disc number. I mostly use rateyourmusic.com and submithub.com to figure out the genre.

Feel welcome to check my Orpheus on Google Drive.
